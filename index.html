    • SYMPHONY project

    I was awarded a prestigous ERC Starting Grant, but the UK was not associated to Horizon Europe framework. Therefore, I received equal funding from UK Research and Innovation (UKRI) for my SYMPHONY project at Newcastle University as a Frontier Research Grant (2023-2028; grant agreement number [EP/Y031059/1]), which combines TESS photometry and spectroscopy to perform asteroseismology of massive stars and constrain their uncertain interior physical processes. All project updates, personnel and outputs are listed on the SYMPHONY project website.
